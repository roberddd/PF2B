    Support and Feedback

    If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for new features, please contact me primarily via the SuperHive (BlenderMarket) inbox or by email (for Gumroad buyers). Alternatively, you may use the Issues and Discussions pages on GitHub.

    Updates¶

    My priority is ensuring compatibility with Blender's Long-Term Support (LTS) releases, but I will also strive to maintain functionality with other Blender updates whenever possible. For details on the current version, check the Changelog.
